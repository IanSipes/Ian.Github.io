How to Join

In order to join this organization, you will need to meet several requirements. You will need an ID, your experience in the real world, and your resume. you will then contact a member, rather it be a phone call or meeting in person, and give the information that they want. Once your done, you will then receive a letter that asks you more questions. You answer yes and no and agree with our Terms of Service. Then you sign the paper and then mail it back to us and receive a confirmation within a week or two.
